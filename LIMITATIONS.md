# Technical Limitations

Public RSIF semantic documentation describes structured quantitative imaging concepts only.

---

# Quantitative Imaging Variability

Quantitative CT parameters may vary depending on:

- scanner manufacturer;
- acquisition protocol;
- reconstruction kernel;
- slice thickness;
- contrast enhancement;
- ROI placement;
- motion artifacts;
- metal artifacts;
- image noise.

---

# Semantic Scope

RSIF documentation describes technical imaging semantics only.

Public repositories do not provide:

- diagnosis;
- disease grading;
- treatment recommendation;
- clinical decision support;
- emergency interpretation.

---

# Public Documentation Notice

Examples and semantic objects contained in public repositories are synthetic documentation examples only.
